## ðŸŒŸ **5. Findings & Recommendations**

  

### **Finding 1 & Recommendation**

  

> [!NOTE] Finding 1Â Â 

> **Finding**: Couriers prioritize efficiency and ease of finding content, forming groups based on frequency, category, and severity during card sorting.Â Â 

> **Recommendation**: High priority to redesign Layer 1 by grouping content based on couriers' preferences to enhance flexibility and speed.Â Â 

### **Finding 2 & Recommendation**

> [!NOTE] Finding 2Â Â 

> **Finding**: The wording used in the contact tree is not understandable, adding cognitive load.Â Â 

> **Recommendation**: High priority to use familiar words and consistent phrasing, tailored to delivery phases, to reduce cognitive strain on couriers.Â Â 

  

### **Finding 3 & Recommendation**

  

> [!NOTE] Finding 3Â Â 

> **Finding**: The process for changing addresses at delivery points is unclear, with multiple entry points leading to inefficiency.Â Â 

> **Recommendation**: Medium priority to streamline by creating a unified flow for address changes and potentially improving self-service solutions.Â Â
